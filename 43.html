Determine the number of units of different products which should be produced and

Diet problems
o
Determine the amount of different kinds of constituents/nutrients which should be
included in a diet so as to minimise the cost of the desired diet such that it contains a
certain minimum amount of each constituent/nutrients.

Transportation problems
o
Determine a transportation schedule in order to find the cheapest way of transporting
