o
The probability P(Ei) is called the priori probability of the hypothesis Ei
o
The conditional probability P(Ei |A) is called a posteriori probability of the hypothesis Ei.
o
Also called the formula for the probability of "causes". Since the Ei's are a partition of the sample
space S, one and only one of the events Ei occurs (i.e. one of the events E i must occur and only
one can occur). Hence, the above formula gives us the probability of a particular Ei, given that
the event A has occurred.
